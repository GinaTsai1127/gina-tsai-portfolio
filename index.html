👤  About me

The analyst who bridges
data and real-world action.

Originally from Taiwan and now based in Berlin, I have built my career helping organisations use data to improve customer experiences, operational performance, and business decision-making. Over the past four years, I’ve worked across SaaS and e-commerce environments, combining analytical thinking with a deep understanding of customer needs.

Today, I’m expanding my focus into AI, automation, and intelligent workflows. I’m particularly interested in how AI can help organisations scale operations, improve customer experiences, and support better decision-making.

Automating customer sentiment analysis and reporting pipelines

Built an end-to-end automated workflow using n8n to ingest, analyze, and report on customer feedback. The pipeline fetches Google Maps reviews, cleans the text, and leverages OpenAI (LLM) to classify reviews into topic categories. It combines this AI classification with user rating data (1–5 stars) to store structured data in Google Sheets, aggregate category-level insights, generate dynamic charts via the QuickChart API, and distribute automated performance reports via Gmail.
